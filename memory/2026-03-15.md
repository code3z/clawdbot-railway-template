### v9 inference: between-market probability math (Ian corrected)
- The model doesn't know about buckets — just predicts P(0), P(1), P(2+)
- For NO on between bucket that is N°F above running max hi_f:
  - N=1: P(wrong) = P(+1) + P(2+) — conservative upper bound
  - N=2: P(wrong) = P(2+)
  - N=3+: skip (illiquid, no return)
- P(NO wins) = 1 − P(wrong)
- This is practically sufficient because high-increment markets are illiquid
- `_compute_trade_p_v9()` implements all three strike types (greater, less, between)
