### Built Backtesting Framework
- `polymarket/data.py` — market/price data fetcher with caching
- `polymarket/backtest.py` — full backtesting engine (fees, circuit breakers, Sharpe, drawdown)
- `polymarket/strategies.py` — 4 strategies: mean_reversion, contrarian, momentum, late_entry
- `polymarket/honest_backtest.py` — unbiased framework with train/test split, Monte Carlo
- `polymarket/fetch_recent.py` — fetches recently resolved markets with price history

### Dataset
- 228 recently resolved markets (119 crypto, 62 other, 29 sports, 12 weather, 6 politics)
- 102 YES / 126 NO resolution — balanced
- Split: 136 dev / 91 validation (sealed)

### Dev Set Results (honest, no peeking at validation)
- **Contrarian is the winner**: +2.10% avg ROI, 43% win rate, $286 total P&L across 136 markets
- Monte Carlo: 85% profitable, median +2.0% ROI, robust ✓
- Mean reversion: slightly negative (-0.57%), needs tuning
- Momentum: poor (-2.81%), doesn't fit prediction markets well
- Late entry: slightly negative (-0.71%), conservative but not profitable yet

### Paper Trader Built & Running Live ⭐
- `polymarket/paper_trader.py` — LIVE, monitoring Binance US + Polymarket in real-time
- **Markets run 24/7** (~1,570/day, every hour, every 5 min) — NOT limited to evening hours
