## Lesson from Ian (6:06 PM ET)
**"Always be skeptical of 1¢ deals. There is a chance we have information no one else has, but it's unlikely — especially in the weather markets."**

The 1¢ price is a RED FLAG, not an opportunity. When a market is at 1¢:
- Either the market knows something we don't (usually this)
- Or it's deeply ITM/OTM and already fully priced in (no profit on the other side)
- Or our direction interpretation is WRONG (as happened today — T61 'less' bought as if it were 'greater')

Weather markets specifically: we are NOT the only ones with access to NWS CLI reports. The market updates quickly. 1¢ almost always means "the market already knows this is a loser."
