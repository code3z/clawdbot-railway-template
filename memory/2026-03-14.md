## Session: Sat Mar 14 morning ‚Äî Overnight Between-Bucket Dip Strategy Research

### Research summary
Investigated why KXHIGHPHIL-26MAR12-B68.5 (winner, floor=68¬∞F cap=69¬∞F) traded at 4-8¬¢ ask overnight before jumping to 50¬¢+ at 1am EDT (midnight LST). Result: systematic mispricing across ALL 20 cities, every week.

### Key findings (backtest: 960 between-markets, Mar 1-13)
- **Corrected window**: noon LST (day-before) ‚Üí midnight LST (start of Kalshi day). Use `td.replace(hour=-lst_off, tzinfo=UTC)` NOT `(td+1).replace(...)`.
- **Candlestick API**: use `yes_ask["low_dollars"]` for intra-minute min ask. `close_dollars` is NOT `close`. Minute-level (`period_interval=1`) required ‚Äî hourly misses intraday lows.
- **Unfiltered base rates**: 3-5c = +50% EV, 5-8c = +55% EV. All buckets positive EV.
- **OM historical forecast gate (‚â§3¬∞F from bucket midpoint)**: 3-5c ‚Üí +426% EV, 5-8c ‚Üí +266% EV. Massive improvement.
- **Adjacent bucket rank gate**: bucket in top-50% by price among its event ‚Üí 5-8c +373% EV.
- **Tighter gating (‚â§2¬∞F) hurts**: samples too small, OM has ~2-3¬∞F inherent uncertainty.
- **Optimal parameters**: YES ask ‚â§10¬¢, OM forecast within 4-5¬∞F of bucket midpoint, GTD expiry at midnight LST.

### 3¬¢ GTD limit order analysis
- 154 markets dipped to ‚â§3¬¢ over 13 days (~12/day). Win rate 3.9%, EV +30% blind.
- Forecast gating barely helps at 3¬¢ (forecast is always far from 3¬¢ buckets by definition).
- Verdict: 3¬¢ GTD blind is mildly +EV but not the main opportunity. 5-10¬¢ with OM gate is the target.
