### OBS Exit Monitor — NEW DAEMON (critical)
- Ian: "You seem not to understand the importance of being first. Most trades must be made in minutes, sometimes in seconds."
- Built `obs_exit_monitor.py` — continuous daemon, polls every 5 min (ASOS) or 60 min (KNYC)
- Finds `min_lower_f` = minimum possible actual °F from reported °C (conservative floor)
- ASOS: whole °C → c_prec=0.5, f_prec=1.0 → ±1°F spread
- KNYC: 0.1°C (NOT same as ASOS) → c_prec=0.05, f_prec=0.1 → ±0.1°F spread (negligible)
- Hard exits: NO on greater (min_lower_f > floor_strike) | YES on between (min_lower_f > cap_strike)
- Only checks `target_date == local_today` — don't apply today's obs to tomorrow's markets
- launchctl: `com.trady.obs-exit-monitor` (KeepAlive=true, RunAtLoad=true)
- Logs: `forecast_logs/obs_exit_monitor.stdout.log`
- Position updater (6x/day) handles soft forecast-based exits; obs monitor handles hard exits
