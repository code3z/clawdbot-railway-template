### Why NWS Obs Trader Placed 0 Trades Today
- 19 markets were mathematically confirmed correct — but ALL priced at 100¢ already

### Key Market Case Studies

**Denver B65.5 (floor=65, cap=66, high=67°F):**
- ASOS showed 19°C = [66,67]°F from 1:30 PM MT — never went above 19°C
- 67°F actual → 19.44°C → rounds to 19°C → max_lower=66 = cap=66 → old code: 66>66=False → missed
- Market was volatile 1:30–4:16 PM MT (2h45m window), hit 83¢ YES at 2:24 PM when traders forgot daily max was sticky
- Our code tracks daily max (not current), so we would have correctly known max was still 19°C during the 18°C dip
- Collapse at 4:16 PM MT (6:16 PM ET) — not bots, human traders with better data
- Kalshi settles from CLI (direct °F), not ASOS — the ambiguity is only in our NWS API data

**SFO B62.5 (cap=63, high=64°F):**
- Obs first showed 18°C = max_lower=64 at 1:05 PM PT (4:05 PM ET)
- max_lower=64 > cap=63 → WOULD have been confirmed NO — but 1 minute before CLI and market collapse
- CLI and market repriced simultaneously at 4:06 PM ET. Very tight window.
- "Below floor NO" (B66.5) rejected: at 1 PM PT day wasn't over — 66°F could still be reached

**Las Vegas (high=77°F per CLI):**
- ASOS showed max 24°C = [75,76]°F ALL DAY — 25°C (77°F) never appeared in 5-minute data
- 5-minute ASOS missed a 1-minute peak (LCD caught it, ASOS didn't)
- Fundamental limitation: 5-min obs can miss peaks. Our strategy is blind to these.
- This is also why "below floor NO" is unsafe without is_day_over()

### Confirmed Decisions / Lessons
- **"Below floor NO" NOT implemented**: by the time is_day_over() fires, market has repriced. No edge window. Also unsafe because 5-min obs can miss higher readings.
- **Kalshi settles from CLI**: no LCD rounding drift concern. The 1°F buffer in between-YES was wrong — the c_to_possible_f range IS the buffer. Double-counted.
- **CONFIRMED_PROB=0.97 was wrong**: caused negative edge on 95-97¢ entries. Confirmed trades should use our_p=1.0.
- **MIN_EDGE=0.10 wrong for confirmed trades**: should be entry < 98¢ (anything below par on a confirmed outcome is profitable)
- **between YES strict inequalities were wrong**: settlement is inclusive (64°F on a 64-65 market = YES). Use >=floor and <=cap.
- **30% boundary tier**: when max_lower==cap (one possible F is YES, one is NO), use our_p=0.30 and trade if edge ≥ 5%
