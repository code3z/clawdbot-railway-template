When presenting any backtest or simulation to Ian, ALWAYS lead with:
- What data granularity was used (1-min, 5-min, hourly, daily)
- What the fill assumption is (filled at close? at ask? at bid? assumed vs real)

**Why copy trading doesn't work:**
- Markets reprice within seconds/minutes of a trade
- By the time you see the trade, the information is already priced in
