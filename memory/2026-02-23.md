### Key Design Decisions
- Real ensemble probabilities: linear interpolation on min/p10/p25/p50/p75/p90/max — no normal CDF assumption
- 100% ensemble coverage rule: skip market if any of 4 models (GFS/ECMWF/ICON/GEM) is missing
- Best-edge-per-city: only take highest edge trade per city/date/measure
- One trade per ticker, no repositioning, no same-day markets
- 8% min edge, 0.25x fractional Kelly, 5% hard cap
